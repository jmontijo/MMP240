A Little About Our Shop

Smashies is a small husband and wife business located in East Harlem of Manhattan. We believe our sandwiches speak for themselves and we challenge any sceptic to say otherwise. We dedicate ourselves to being the breakfast choice for early morning commuters and the sandwich spot for afternoon school kids. We provide the most delicious sandwiches and freshly squeezed, freshly blended juices.
